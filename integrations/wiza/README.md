# Wiza

Find and verify B2B contact information using LinkedIn data. Enrich individual contacts or bulk lists with verified email addresses, phone numbers, LinkedIn profile details, and company firmographics. Look up contacts by LinkedIn URL, name and company, or email address. Retrieve over 50 data points including work history, skills, education, company industry, headcount, revenue, and funding details. Check credit balance and receive webhook notifications when enrichment completes.
