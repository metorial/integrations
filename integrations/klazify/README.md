# Klazify

Classify and categorize any website, domain, IP address, or email into structured business data. Retrieve company profiles including revenue, employee range, industry classification, HQ location, and tech stack. Look up brand logos, social media links, competitor domains, domain registration data, and parked domain status. Supports IAB Content Taxonomy with 620+ categories and confidence scores. Enrich data in batch via CSV upload.
