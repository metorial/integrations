# Dataforseo

Retrieve SEO, SEM, and digital marketing data from search engines, marketplaces, and websites. Search SERP results for any keyword, location, language, and search engine (Google, Bing, YouTube, Yahoo, Baidu). Obtain keyword metrics including search volume, CPC, competition, and keyword suggestions. Analyze backlink profiles, referring domains, and link intersections for competitor gap analysis. Crawl websites for technical SEO audits with JavaScript rendering support. Look up domain Whois data and technology stack detection. Retrieve business reviews and social media interaction data from Google Business Profile, Facebook, Pinterest, and Reddit. Monitor ecommerce product listings, pricing, and seller data from Google Shopping and Amazon. Get app store data and reviews from Google Play and App Store. Perform content analysis with sentiment detection and brand mention monitoring. Generate AI-optimized responses from LLMs (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity) and retrieve AI-specific keyword search volume. Supports real-time (Live) and asynchronous (Standard) data retrieval with webhook/pingback task completion notifications.
