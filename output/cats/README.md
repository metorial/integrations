# <img src="logo.png" height="20"> Cats

Manage the full recruitment lifecycle with an applicant tracking system. Create, update, search, and delete candidate records with resume uploads and parsing. Manage job orders with workflows, statuses, and career portal publishing. Track companies and contacts as a recruiting CRM. Build and manage candidate pipelines through configurable hiring workflow stages. Log activities (calls, emails, meetings) and create tasks against candidates, contacts, or jobs. Organize records with tags, lists, and custom fields. Perform advanced filtered searches with boolean logic and geo-distance queries. Subscribe to webhooks or poll events for changes to candidates, jobs, contacts, companies, pipelines, activities, and users. Initiate and retrieve full data backups.
