# Headout

Search and book tours, activities, attractions, and experiences in cities worldwide. Browse products by city, category, collection, or subcategory. Retrieve detailed product information including images, descriptions, reviews, variants, and pricing. Check real-time inventory and availability for specific dates and times. Create bookings with customer and passenger details. Access localized content in multiple languages and currencies.
